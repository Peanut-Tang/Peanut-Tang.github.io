Research Interest

My research interests broadly lie in theoretical computer science, especially pseudorandomness, computational complexity, and cryptography. I have worked on PRG design, hash functions, and coding. I am also passionate about combinatorics and probability in pure mathematics, as well as optimization problems in computational and financial mathematics.
