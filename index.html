About me

I am a senior student majoring in computer science and technology in the School of the Gifted Young, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I'm fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xue Chen.

Overall, I have a broad interest in theoretical computer science, particularly in areas related to pseudorandomness, computational complexity and cryptography. I have conducted research on the design of PRGs, hash functions, and coding. At the same time, I am passionate about exploring combinatorics and probability in pure mathematics, as well as optimization problems in computational or financial mathematics.
